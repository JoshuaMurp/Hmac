About Us

Welcome to HMAC, Inverness's hub for MMA passion and community. More than a training ground, HMAC is a family where experienced trainers guide fighters of all levels with dedication. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a newcomer, our inclusive environment is tailored for everyone. Join HMAC for a journey of self-improvement, teamwork, and the thrill of the fight—where every punch thrown is a step toward personal growth and victory.
